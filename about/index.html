The Architectural Committee

An Architectural Committee (AC), composed of volunteer residents, acts to provide feedback on elements defined in the DC&Rs, as well as approve plans for new development in collaboration with relevant neighbors. The current AC was elected in 2022 and are expected to serve for a three year term.

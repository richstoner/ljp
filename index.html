La Jolla Palisades I

Welcome to our community

La Jolla Palisades I is a hilltop community in La Jolla, CA established in 1958 as a subdivision of the City of San Diego. We've created this website as a forum for communication and to foster relationships within our neighborhood. The community is supported by resident volunteers working to ensure the community is a safe and wonderful place to call home.
